About This Page

This page is an online resume.
The timeline shows the full list of achievements and projects I have done so far.
By clicking on them, it will show the details of the item you have selected.

The main highlight of the page is the sorting capability:
You can click on the sort dropdown and add up to 5 properties you care about.
Of course, you can assign different weights for each properties.
The page will then refresh, showing the most relevant item first.
You can check its relevance by hovering over the carousel information tab (that is, the small dots on the slider).
The whiter the dot is, the higher the relevance of that particular item.

Scope refers to how much of the project relies on (one or more) of the properties selected.
Lower scope means that the property plays a less important role on this particular item.
An item can have a high scope but low relevance when the project has a low complexity.
For example, a simple bootstrap demo page can have 100% scope for webdev, but only counts as being 5% relevant.

This page is powered by Bootstrap, FontAwesome, JQuery, jqtimeline, and subtlepatterns.
And of course, github, for allowing me to host this mini project.
