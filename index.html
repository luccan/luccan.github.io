About This Page

This page is a smart resume. The idea was to have a dynamic resume to satisfy various employers.


The main highlight of this page is its sorting capability:

  • You can click on the sort dropdown and add up to 5 properties you care about.
  • Of course, you can assign different importance for each properties.

    • Relevance and Scope

  • You can hover over the dots on the carousel.
  • The whiter the dot is, the higher the relevance of that particular item to the properties you selected.
    • Scope refers to how much of the project relies on (one or more) of the properties selected.
    An item can have a high scope but low relevance when the project has a low complexity.
    For example, a simple bootstrap demo page can have 100% scope for webdev, but only counts as being 5% relevant.
